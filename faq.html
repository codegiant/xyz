Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can i get ROOM ID or PASSWORD?

➤ ROOM ID or PASSWORD will be issued on WhatAapp and TournaGround App.

2. What if i paid for tournament and could not join the match?

➤ Check the time and date of the match carefully before joining the match, and join the ROOM 15 minutes before the match starts.
If you have joined the match then you should have to play the match.

3. If the game crashed in the middle of the match?

➤ That match will be counted in fair play.

4. What to do if someone is cheating in game?

➤ Take a screenshort of that player's profile or name, ID something and send us with your word.
(You can directly leave a message anytime on WhatsApp) Feel free to contact us.

5. Where and when will the won money come?

➤ Winning amount will be sent to your Paytm Wallet after 10 minutes of the match.

6. Provision of refund!

➤ Refunds can be applied 30 minutes before the match starts, once the match starts, your amount will not be refunded.

7. How can i use my ROOM CARD here?

➤ You have to contact us for using your ROOM CARD on TournaGround.
